Virtual reality

VR creates fully immersive, lifelike 3D worlds that users can explore as if they were physically present

Users can interact with objects and environments using controllers, hand gestures, or even their own movements.

VR provides real-time feedback and updates to user actions, creating a seamless and responsive experience.

Through visuals, sound, and sometimes haptic feedback (like vibrations), VR engages multiple senses to enhance realism.
